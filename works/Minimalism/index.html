When I visited a rural area of Uttar Pardedh I found this Village which thrilled me to do a project that can really change the situation of people in that area . This is a village where only Beggers live and they train their kids to go out and beg for food and money. I just wanted to take the picture of the kids who never goes to school but they start living the professional life from the begining. This is what I called "begining of begging".
